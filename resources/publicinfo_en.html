Apparatus for local blowing KRIOSAN is appropriate for a cryostimulation action in temperature – 150 oC. KRIOSAN finds application in:

  • RHEUMATOLOGY
  • ORTHOPEDIA
  • NEUROLOGY
  • DERMATOLOGY
  • SPORT MEDICINE

Cryostimulation is a surface application of cryogenic temperatures lower than - 100 oC. The brief application (2 – 4 minutes) of such low temperatures causes a physiological reaction which assists in the treatment of motor and spinal diseases.
