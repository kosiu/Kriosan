Apparatus for local blowing KRIOSAN is appropriate for a cryostimulation action in temperature -150oC. KRIOSAN finds application in:

  • RHEUMATOLOGY
  • ORTHOPEDIA
  • NEUROLOGY
  • DERMATOLOGY
  • SPORT MEDICINE

Cryostimulation is a surface application of cryogenic temperatures lower than -100oC. The brief application (2 – 4 minutes) of such low temperatures causes a physiological reaction which assists in the treatment of motor and spinal diseases.

Technical data of KRIOSAN

  • outlet temperature of nitrogen vapour: -150oC
  • average consumption of liquid nitrogen during operation: 0,08 – 0,11 kg LN2/min
  • weight of installation (without liquid nitrogen): 26kg
  • full weight of installation (including liquid nitrogen): 52kg
